Trevo Account Deletion

Request deletion of your account and all associated data

Delete Your Trevo Account

You can request the deletion of your Trevo account and all associated data at any time. Once processed, your account will be permanently removed and you will no longer be able to sign in or access your groups.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option A: In-App

If available in your version of the app:

  1. Open Trevo and go to Settings
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option B: Email Request

Send an email to:

mielforgelabs@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your username or phone number associated with your account
  • The email address linked to your Trevo account (if applicable)

Use the subject line:

Subject: Account Deletion Request — [Your Username or Phone Number]

What Data Is Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (display name, status, preferences)
  • Location data and location history
  • Group memberships and group-related data
  • All active sessions and authentication tokens
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention

Some limited information may be retained where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes. Any retained data will be handled in accordance with applicable regulations and will not be used for marketing or profiling.

Processing Time

Your account will be permanently deleted within 7 business days of receiving a valid request. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at mielforgelabs@gmail.com.
