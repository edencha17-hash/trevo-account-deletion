Trevo Account Deletion

Request deletion of your Trevo account and associated personal data

Delete Your Trevo Account

You can request deletion of your Trevo account at any time. Once your request is processed, you will no longer be able to sign in to Trevo or access account-related features.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request account deletion, send an email to:

mielforgelabs@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your phone number, if relevant to account identification
  • Any additional details that may help us identify your account

Suggested email subject:

Account Deletion Request — [Your Email or Phone Number]

What Will Be Deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we will delete or de-identify your account-related personal data, which may include:

  • Account profile information
  • Active sessions and authentication access
  • Location-related data associated with your account
  • Group membership data associated with your account, subject to system and legal constraints

Data That May Be Retained

Some limited information may be retained for legal, security, fraud-prevention, abuse-prevention, or operational reasons. Backup copies may also persist for a limited period before automatic deletion.

Processing Time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within 7 business days.

Questions

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact:

mielforgelabs@gmail.com
